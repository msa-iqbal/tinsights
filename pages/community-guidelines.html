Community Guidelines

Last updated: June 28, 2025

1. Welcome to the T-Insights Community

At T-Insights, we aim to build a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative space for developers, designers, tech enthusiasts, and learners. These Community Guidelines help ensure that everyone feels safe and valued while sharing knowledge, code, opinions, and feedback.

2. Be Respectful and Supportive

Treat everyone with respect. Healthy debate is encouraged, but personal attacks, discrimination, or harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.

  • Use kind and inclusive language
  • Disagree respectfully and constructively
  • Support newcomers and celebrate learning

3. Keep Content Relevant and Valuable

Contribute meaningfully. Share resources, projects, code snippets, tutorials, questions, and discussions that add value to the community.

  • Stay on topic in threads and discussions
  • Avoid low-effort content, spam, or self-promotion
  • Cite sources and give credit where it’s due

4. No Hate Speech, Harassment, or Discrimination

We have zero tolerance for hate speech, bullying, or targeted harassment. Everyone is welcome here regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

5. Protect Privacy and Security

Respect the privacy of others and avoid sharing personal, sensitive, or private information without consent.

  • Don’t post private emails, passwords, or API keys
  • Report security vulnerabilities responsibly
  • Avoid phishing, scams, or misleading content

6. Collaborate and Contribute Constructively

Share your expertise, help others grow, and collaborate on projects with a community-first mindset. Avoid being dismissive or condescending.

7. No Spam or Unsolicited Promotion

T-Insights is not a place for self-promotion, affiliate links, or advertisements unless clearly permitted in specific threads.

8. Reporting and Moderation

If you see behavior that violates these guidelines, report it to the moderators. We reserve the right to remove content or accounts that violate our rules, with or without warning.

9. Contributor Etiquette

When contributing content or code:

  • Use clear titles and descriptions
  • Format code properly and test before sharing
  • Respect licensing and usage terms of third-party tools

10. Keep Improving the Community

We believe in continuous improvement. If you have suggestions to enhance the platform or its culture, we welcome your feedback.

11. Contact and Support

Need help or have a concern? Email us at sayhi.msg@gmail.com and we’ll do our best to assist you.
